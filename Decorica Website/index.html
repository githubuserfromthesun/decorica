intro

Decorica Co. is engaged in the hand sanitizer manufacturing industry. We are in this business to manufacture and to package hand sanitizers for wholesale distribution at very economical prices. Our target markets are: hospitals and medical facilities, pharmacies, supermarkets, schools, government offices, hotels, business premises, households, and everyone in our target market location. Aside from creating the excellent quality of hand sanitizers in different forms, our customer’s safety is our utmost consideration. Our customers’ health and care are the reason why we are in this business, so we exert more effort to satisfy them when they purchase our hand sanitizers.

Decorica Hand Sanitizer

The company offers Decorica hand sanitizers in the form of gel, foam, and liquid that instantly disinfect hands by killing viruses and bacteria to help control the spread of infectious diseases such as flu, SARS, common cold, diarrhea, and COVID-19. We are focusing on the production of safe and environment-friendly product variants. Our hand sanitizers are time-efficient, convenient to use, and available in pocket-size squeeze bottles, sprays, pumps, touchless and smart dispensers, as well as 5-Liter size of containers.

The escalating awareness about the significance of maintaining hand hygiene, owing to the sudden outbreak of the COVID-19 pandemic, is among the primary factors driving the hand sanitizer market. Current increases in demand for this product created opportunities for the company to produce hand sanitizers for people who are taking extra precautions to avoid getting infected by this deadly virus. Using a Decorica Hand Sanitizer, in the absence of soap and water, is one of the best ways to disinfect hands from harmful microorganisms.

About

Decorica Co. is a company that manufactures hand sanitizers in a variety of forms, including gel, foam, and liquid. Our vision is to establish a standard hand sanitizer production company that will achieve a major position in the hand sanitation industry. Our mission is to manufacture and supply hand sanitizer products which meet the highest quality, safety and efficiency standards, at reasonable and competitive prices. The company values cleanliness and believes that cleanliness is one of the foundations of good health. Good health is the best wealth; it is a priceless asset that anyone can own; and it is the greatest blessing that can be enjoyed in this world. Decorica Co. is a company that is committed to develop and innovate safe and effective hand sanitizers that help in minimizing and controlling the spread of infectious diseases that endanger people’s lives. The company was founded in 2022 by Kierra Zimbelman, Heart Malazarte, Ansherina Nolasco, Rhiene Vina, Rashid Ishmail Mangulabnan, and Keanu Andrei Bulasa. Their friendship and their trust in each other are what gave birth to the idea of putting up a business together. Their goal is to build a solid foundation that will strengthen this company, to make profits, and at the same time give their customers the value for their money.
